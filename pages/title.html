Barrio Suburbanism

Reshaping Metropolitan Geographies

Building upon urban studies of public policy and planning's roles in the formation of the barrio, we turn our attention to how suburban forms operate in a multiracial and regional context. This interactive map displays student investagations into how Chicano/a and Latino/a populations have impacted the econcomic, social, and spatial contours of suburbs in Greater Los Angeles
